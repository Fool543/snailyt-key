How Ads Help Developers

Many developers provide free software and services, but ads help them sustain their projects and continue development.

Why Are Ads Important?

Ads generate revenue that supports independent developers, funds new features, and keeps content accessible for free.

Without ads, many developers would have to charge users directly, making software less accessible.

Ads allow developers to focus on improving their projects, rather than worrying about monetization through paid subscriptions.

By simply viewing or interacting with ads, users contribute to the growth of the software they use daily.
